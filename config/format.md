# Output format

## Digest header
*🗞 [Morning/Evening] Digest — [Day DD Mon]*
_[N] emails scanned · [N] articles curated_

One blank line after the header before the first category.

## Categories
Use these categories in this order. Skip a category entirely 
if there are no items for it:
1. AI & Tech
2. Markets & Finance
3. B2B & GTM
4. Culture & Society
5. Ideas & Essays

## Item format
Each item must follow this structure exactly:

- *[Article title](resolvedUrl)*
  [Line 1: what this article is about — specific, one sentence]
  [Line 2: why you should read it — the angle, the insight, the stakes]

One blank line between items within the same category.
One blank line + bold category header between categories.

## Synopsis rules
- 2 lines maximum per item
- Line 1: what it is — factual, specific, no vague openers
- Line 2: why it matters to me — direct, no filler phrases
- Never start with "In this article", "The author argues", "This piece"
- Write like a trusted researcher briefing a busy founder

## Constraints
- 7 to 10 items per digest
- On slow days send whatever is available, even 2 or 3 items
- Never modify or reconstruct URLs — use resolvedUrl exactly
- Bold category headers, bold+linked article titles
- No emoji anywhere except the single 🗞 in the header
